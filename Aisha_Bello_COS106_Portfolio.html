Brief Biography

My name is Aisha Bello and I am from Abuja, Nigeria. I am currently in my first year studying Computer Science at MIVA Open University. Before university, I attended Government Secondary School in Abuja where I developed a strong interest in mathematics and problem-solving.

I chose Computer Science because I have always been curious about how technology works. I want to understand how apps and websites are made, and eventually build products that can help people across Nigeria and Africa. Right now I am still learning the basics, but I am working hard every day to improve my skills.
